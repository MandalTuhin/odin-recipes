Chicken Curry

Make Chicken curry

How to make amazing and astounding chicken curry? There are actually no secrets.. you just have to add the right ingredients and follow the right steps and ... you have a chicken curry.

Not only is it good for the taste, it also has several health benefits. the chicken meat has several nutrients and vitamins that are essential to the body.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Meat
  • Garlic Paste
  • Chopped onions
  • Butter
  • Masalas

Steps:

  1. Clean the chicken meat.
  2. Marinate it if you want extra flavours.
  3. Heat the kadhai & let the butter melt
  4. Stir fry the chopped onions untill they are brown.
  5. Add the Garlic paste.
  6. Add the chicken.
  7. Let it fry and cover it so that flavours don't get lost.
  8. Add the Masalas after 10 minutes.
  9. Cover again and let it cook for another 10-15 minutes.
  10. Serve and Enjoy your Chicken Curry.
