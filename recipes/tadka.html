How to make brilliant Tadka

Making Brilliant Tadka sabji is actually easier than it seems. Tadkas are full of anti-oxidants and have essential nutrients packed in them.

We are going to look at how to make them easily...

Ingredients:

  • Tadka!
  • Butter
  • Pepper
  • Paanch Phodon

Steps:

  1. Let your Tadka sit in water for the night.
  2. Heat a pan and spread the Butter.
  3. Add the Paanch Phodon and let it turn brown.(The ingridents here an jump on you. be a little cautious but dont be discouraged by it.
  4. Add the moistened Tadka.
  5. Add peper & cover the kadhai with a lid.
  6. Wait 10 mins.
  7. Your Tadka is ready.
