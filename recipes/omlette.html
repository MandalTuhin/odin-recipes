Omelette

Build the amazing omlettes

How to make the perfect omlettes? if you ask me, I will say experiment with it, try to do different things, mix some sugar, throw some garlic, even some tomato ketchup. Break the rules!

Because Cooking is an art and is creativity. Rules are made to be broken. Enjoy your omlette.

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Chopped Onions
  • Butter
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Break the egg into a bowl.
  2. Chop some onions, if you haven't.
  3. Heat the pan and let the butter spread and melt.
  4. Mix salt in the bowl.
  5. Fry the onions on the pan untill brown.
  6. Spread the Egg onto the pan.
  7. Enjoy your Omelette!
